Immediate Help & Emergency Resources

Getting Help Fast

These resources are best for urgent or same-day needs. For life-threatening emergencies, call 911.

Crisis and urgent support

Direct services for mental health emergencies, personal safety, and housing crisis.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
Free 24/7 support for mental health, emotional distress, or substance use concerns. Call or text 988 for immediate help.
Availability: 24/7
Cost: Free
Call 988 Learn More
Turning Point Crisis Services
Emergency crisis support for survivors of domestic violence, sexual assault, and child abuse in Union County. Trauma-informed services 24/7.
Domestic Violence: 704-283-7233
Sexual Assault: 704-283-7770
Location: 501 South Main St, Waxhaw
Availability: 24/7
Call Now Learn More
Union County Community Shelter
Emergency housing, hot meals, and rehousing support for families facing homelessness. Available 24/7, walk-ins welcome.
Phone: 704-289-5300
Location: Union County
Availability: 24/7
Call Now Learn More

Same-day help

Food, basic services, and healthcare access that can start today.

Common Heart Food Pantries
Common Heart operates food pantries in Union County and provides groceries to help stabilize families living in poverty.
Service Area: Union County
Note: Check the official pantry schedule for current locations and times
Common Cupboard Delivery
Union County residents who cannot get to an evening pantry because of a significant barrier may qualify for Saturday morning grocery delivery.
Availability: Saturday morning delivery
Eligibility: Union County residents with transportation or access barriers
HealthQuest
Connects uninsured residents with medications and health education.
