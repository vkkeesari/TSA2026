Why Register Through The Hub

Hosting a drive can be overwhelming, so our platform acts as your Logistics Partner.

When you register a drive through the Weddington-Waxhaw Hub, we provide promotion, supplies, coordination, and verification support from start to finish.

  • Promotion: We feature your drive on our Urgent Needs ticker and community calendar.
  • Supplies: Access to printable flyer templates and social media graphics tailored to our town's aesthetic.
  • Coordination: We bridge the gap between your collection and the final delivery to a trusted organization.
  • Verification: We provide a Certificate of Impact for student service hours or corporate social responsibility records.
