Bridging Generations

Our mission is to ensure that no senior in the Weddington-Waxhaw area feels isolated or overwhelmed by modern challenges. We provide a secure platform where seniors can request Micro-Supports, small tasks that make a big difference, fulfilled by verified local volunteers.

Services & Support Categories

We focus on three primary areas where our community's youth and adults can provide immediate value.

  • Tech & Digital Literacy: Help with setting up new tablets/phones, troubleshooting Zoom for family calls, identifying phishing or scam emails, and basic social media navigation.
  • Household & Yard Assistance: Help with bringing trash bins to the curb, light leaf raking, changing hard-to-reach lightbulbs, or moving small furniture items.
  • Wellness & Companionship: Help with weekly Safety Check phone calls, friendly porch visits, or playing a game of chess/cards at a local park.
